Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of flavor and hearty ingredients. It’s a baked pasta dish that typically consists of several layers of ingredients, including pasta sheets, meat sauce, béchamel (white sauce), and cheese.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely choppe
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef or pork (or a mix)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce
  3. Prepare the Lasagna Noodles
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake the Lasagna
  6. Garnish and Serve

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
